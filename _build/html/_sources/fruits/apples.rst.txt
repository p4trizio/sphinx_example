Apples
=======

The fruit world is awesome!
Apples can be of many varieties

Golden Delicious
----------------
Undoubtedly one of the most important apple varieties of the 20th century, both as a commercial variety in its own right, and as breeding stock for many other varieties. Very good flavor when home-grown.


Granny Smith
------------
The most instantly-recognised of all apples, and perhaps Australia's most famous export.

Origins
~~~~~~~
* Species: Malus domestica
* Parentage: Possibly French Crab and Rome Beauty
* Originates from: Australia

Identification
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
* Fruit colour: Green
* Flesh colour: White to Greenish to Greenish Yellow
* Fruit size: Medium
* Fruit shape: Short-round-conical
